Oh no! The animals have mysteriously gone missing!

Help with the search!


Paw Patrol is a pervasive game, played with your phone in real time. Complete missions in the form of puzzles and hunts in order to best the crafty creatures and capture them once more! The experience should last no more than 45 minutes and is held at the Goldsmith's Library.

What to do


Groups of four!
Come alone or bring your friends to complete missions and find the lost animals :)


The team will consist of 3 normal players and a secret saboteur trying to fulfill their own agenda. Beware! They will try to sabotage your missions to keep the animals free!


One smartphone per person

It is recommend you bring a fully charged phone, or bring along a portable charger.


Don't worry if you're lost, the game masters will be there to encourage you and lend a helping hand throughout the game!
